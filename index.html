What I do

My background is in biological science and public health, and I have experience with biostatistics and research methodologies. I use that knowledge to work towards a world where everyone has a fair opportunity to be healthy.

I am certified in Microsoft Azure and have experience using Azure SQL Server to manage and query relational data in the cloud. I have worked with patient records, resource referral systems, and case management platforms.

I am a Tableau Certified Desktop Specialist and I make interactive visualizations and dashboards to communicate complex data. I also love programming and learning to make new web-based visualizations.
